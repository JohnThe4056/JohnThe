Scratch: Gobo Killer

Description

The game, Gobo Killer, embodies the ideas of World War 1 and 2. Essentially, Gobos invade your home planet, and the player is forced to protect the people of your home planet. The player is forced into a fight or die, which is a reference to Benjaminn Franklin's "Join or Die" slogan for the war of independence. For each Gobo the player kills, points are rewarded to the player on the basis of a time multipler. For the randomness, Gobos spawn at random places across to add an aiming aspect to the game. The game also gets unnecessarily impossible as time progress, however, an upgrade system is implemented to postpone the inevitable defeat. Gobo Killer is a survival game in which the player have 3 lives, that will postpone the inevitable destruction the planet will face.

Reflection

My friend Kent and I created this game because as children, we played many survival flash games that involved "protecting the planet" and being heros.Because of this, we followed our inspiration and sought to recreate essentially our childhoods. This is an algorithm because it automates the spawn timers and positions of the Gbos while in game.
